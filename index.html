Families

In this graph you will discover the family tree of the main Houses in the world of Game of Thrones. For each character displayed, you can see his family ties with his significant other, parents and children. There is the possibility to display the actor IMDB card of the character interpreter. It is important to mention that by clicking on a character card, you are also selecting him on the "Relations Graph" and vice versa.

Relations

This time we will display different relationships between a character (sometimes a wepaon!) and other ones. It goes from a simple family relationship to a more "tragic" relation as who killed who. Select one of the proposed relations, so you can display it and click on a character to highlight its relations with others. By selecting a character the Family graph will highlight its family tree, if it exists.

Timeline

In this graph we will follow our beloved characters on their journey arround Westeros and Essos. We follow each one of them during the TV Show seasons and determine their location, either is it The Wall, The North, Kingslanding or other various places.



Books' Words Importance

Have you ever wandered what are the most common words in each book of A Song Of Ice And Fire, where each chapter represents a character point of view ? This graph answers this question by plotting a words cloud that change dynamically when we select a character or a book volume.
